About

Yoga should feel like belonging,
not performing.

Wyld Spirits Yoga was born out of a simple belief: that yoga belongs to everyone — not just the experienced, the flexible, or the calm.

We've all walked into a yoga class and felt out of place. Too stiff. Too new. Too busy to have shown up with anything resembling inner peace. That feeling shouldn't be part of the practice — and in our classes, it isn't.

Wyld Spirits brings accessible, grounding yoga to communities across the DFW area. Whether it's a winery patio, a CrossFit box, a school gymnasium, your company's wellness day, or your backyard, every session is designed to meet you exactly where you are.

"We believe yoga isn't reserved for the flexible, the experienced, or the perfectly calm. It's for the real, the rushed, and the ready — which is all of us, most of the time."

Why Mobile?

Because the best yoga is the yoga you actually show up for. Removing the barrier of getting to a studio — the commute, the parking, the unfamiliar space — means more people practice. And more people practicing means more community, more wellness, more joy.

We go to CrossFit boxes, wineries, parks, schools, offices, and living rooms. We partner with venues that want to offer their communities something meaningful. We say yes to the unusual requests, because the most memorable sessions usually start with "I have kind of a weird idea..."

What to Expect From Us

Warmth. No judgment. Clear instruction. Genuine care for where you are in your practice and your life. We're not here to perform yoga at you — we're here to practice alongside you.

And sometimes there are goats. Because joy is also part of the practice.
