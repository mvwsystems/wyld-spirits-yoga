About Wyld Spirits

A warm, inclusive space to move, breathe, and grow

Wyld Spirits Yoga brings accessible, grounding yoga to communities across the DFW area. Whether it's a winery patio, a CrossFit box, a school gymnasium, or your own backyard, every session is designed to meet you exactly where you are.

We believe yoga isn't reserved for the flexible, the experienced, or the perfectly calm. It's for the real, the rushed, and the ready — which is all of us, most of the time.
