What to Expect

Your first class

There's no test. No perfect form required. No previous experience needed. Just show up exactly as you are.

Arrive 5–10 minutes early to get settled. Wear whatever you're comfortable moving in. Bring water. Mats are available to rent if you don't have one.

"Every class is a beginning. We meet you there."

Class Length
60 minutes

Each class runs 60 minutes — enough to go deep without going long. Warm-up, flow, and close built in.

All Levels
Truly all levels

We mean it. Modifications offered for every pose. You'll never feel behind or left out.

Equipment
Just yourself

Mat rentals available. Bring water and wear something you can move in. That's it.
